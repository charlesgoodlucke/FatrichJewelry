From Nigeria to Dubai & Beyond

Founded under the vision of Hajia Fatimah (Okandeji Fatimoh Morenike), Fatrich Accessories Trading LLC has grown from a prestigious boutique into a globally recognized luxury jewelry brand.

Our core ethos is **Unalloyed Trust** and premium material integrity. Specializing in Italian 925 sterling silver, Dubai gold, and luxury watches, we deliver high-fashion jewelry with certificate guarantees and a strict **No-Scam policy**.

In 2024, our dedication was recognized at the highest level when we were awarded the **"Outstanding Nigerian Jeweler of the Year in UAE"** by the Iconic Cosmopolitan Business Award.
